## App Store Connect checklist

### App Information (left sidebar)

- [ ] **Privacy Policy URL** → paste privacy URL above
- [ ] **Category** → Business
- [ ] **Content Rights** → does not contain third-party content

### Distribution → iOS App → version page

- [ ] **Screenshots** → upload `AppStoreScreenshots/appstore-6.5/01` through `04` (iPhone 6.5")
- [ ] **Description** → paste from `APP_STORE_COPY.md`
- [ ] **Keywords** → `power bi,fabric,bi,monitoring,refresh,dataset,workspace,analytics,semantic model,alert`
- [ ] **Support URL** → support URL above
- [ ] **Copyright** → `2026 Aurea Quantra`
- [ ] **Build** → select **1.1 (2)** (the uploaded build)
- [ ] **Age Rating** → complete questionnaire (no mature content → 4+)

### App Review Information

- [ ] **Sign-in required?** → No (demo works without sign-in)
- [ ] **Notes** → paste App Review notes from `APP_STORE_COPY.md`
- [ ] **Contact** → your name, phone, email

### Pricing

- [ ] **Price** → Free

---

## Submit

1. Resolve any yellow/red warnings on the version page
2. Click **Add for Review**
3. Click **Submit to App Review**

Review typically takes 24–48 hours. You’ll get email when approved or if Apple has questions.
